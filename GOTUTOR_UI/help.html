How do I ask a question a to the classroom?

Simply navigate to the forum section. Then, you can create a thread by completing the topmost dialogue. The forum is live, so you can be assured you are always seeing the latest posts on the forum.

How do I upload a write-up or an assignment as a tutor?

Navigate to the assignment section and upload a PDF by completing the dialogue. The PDF will be displayed for your students to see.

I want to view or configure the classroom's office hours.

This is possible via the classroom section. Tutors can add or remove office hours. Anyone enrolled in the classroom can view this.

How do I reserve an appointment?

Simply view the appointment calendar under the appointments section. Click on any open space to reserve that spot.
